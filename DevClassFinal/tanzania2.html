Tanzania - Working Conditions & Life

Dar es Salaam has a problem with slums. The poorer residents crowd into downtown areas or large slums, many without running water or basic services. The wealthier live in beachside mansions in the city's northern districts.

Here are some interesting facts and information about Tanzania, Africa, from the website nationsencylopedia.com. Though, this may be twenty year old information. The minimum wage, at that time, was US $30.00 per month. They have a six day work week. There is a 44 to 48 hour work week. According to the article they try to protect children from working at too early an age. Unfortunately, the poor don't seem to be able to be applied by this protection. The same for workplace safety. If they apply the laws, they are in danger of losing their job.
