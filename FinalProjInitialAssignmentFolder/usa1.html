My Work Experience in the US

I have worked and lived in the US all my life. I found that work here depends on some work qualities. It is good to have these qualifications to get a job in the USA.

There seems to be a lot of factors that help with getting good work here. Your self-worth is probably the base on which a job in the US depends on. Lately, I found a new number in the work equation. How much do you support the military. Just look at Colin Kaepernick, he can't get work because of taking a stand on the football field. Of course, your education or skill set is important. What is your ability to do the job right. These things may depend on your vocation. Some jobs just require self-worth.
